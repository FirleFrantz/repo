Keyboard Theme for Laetus, by Spark

Updates on Hold!

Creating these Laetus themes to support a wide range of languages and devices has been a colossal task. It's also been a learning experience as this is a brand new theming experience. 

In the process of supporting many languages, I attempted to take some shortcuts by sharing image files between different languages. This is a pixel precise operation to make sure there's no movement between layouts when switching. These shortcuts I tried created errors. Errors that are hard to spot with only one testing device. 

Switching between language layouts with the current versions of my themes may look jarring at times. Key sizes may differ and or shift positions. As a perfectionist, I just can't stand for this.

I've found where I went wrong, and rather than examining every single file, I'd rather just start over from scratch basically. So that's what I'm doing. Starting over with a different work flow in mind.

Unfortunately this will delay any further releases and updates I had planned. But, in the long run will benefit me in the creation of future themes.

This may take a while, and I may feel burnt out at times. But I will try to stay persistent in making progress.
