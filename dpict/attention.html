Getting this error, when installing?

How to fix the error

Use Filza on your phone (or SSH in to your phone), and navigate to /Library/Themes/

Find and delete folders called...

"Modern13.theme"

"Modern13 Dark.theme"

"Modern13 Gloss.theme"

"Modern13 Light.theme"

You should now be free to install the update without issue.

Why did this happen?

Version 1.5 introduced a new Gloss variant, but was originally packaged outside of Modern13 as it's own theme. In 1.5.1 I merged them in to one package, and now it's conflicting with previous installs of 1.5 or lower. 

The update is trying to dump files in to Library/Themes/Modern13 Gloss/ but cydia is telling us Gloss already exists from another package, and can't be overwritten because it's tied to another package. So manually deleting those files SHOULD fix the issue. PLEASE contact me if you're still having issues after this fix.
