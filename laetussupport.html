Support & Troubleshooting

 • Not compatible with Saw (tweak).

 • Partial support for iKeywi. (Must use Swipe function).

 • Not compatible with tweaks that make non-notch devices act as notch devices.

 Known Issues

 • Manually installing .debs doesn't seem to work for some people. Please just use Zebra installer, if you're on a jailbreak that comes with Sileo.

 • Due to a bug with Sketch.app, some exported canvases might show a blank 1px line at the edge of your device, or between base keyboard image, and predictive bar. Please contact me if you spot this.

 • Due to the aforementioned Sketch.app bug, some keyplanes might shift a pixel between Letters & Numbers. Please contact me if you spot this.

 • Due to the aforementioned Sketch.app bug, some keyplanes show a red line for some stupid reason. Will be fixed soon.
