DEPTHS

Wallpapers for stock iOS to give your icons shadows.

How to apply?

Settings.app>Accessibility>Motion>Reduce Motion (ON)

Then set your wallpaper without making any adjustments.

You can turn Reduce Motion (OFF) when wallpaper is set.

(These wallpapers are experimental, as they haven't been tested on each device personally.)
